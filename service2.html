Hardwood Floors

Floor Refinish

Hardwood Floor Refinishing is an excellent way to restore floors to their original beauty. It is a great way to extend the life of your hardwood floors. As the hardwood flooring gets older over the years, the color fades and changes. Refinishing involves sanding floors down the uppermost layer of the floor and applying fresh stain and lacquer over the existing wood. Equipments you need are drum sander, stain, and lacquer to bring back its natural beauty.
